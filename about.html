Get to know me outside of design!

Hi, I’m Rose—a first-generation Haitian American UX designer with a background in Biology and Chemistry, born and raised in Miami, Florida. My journey into design is rooted in a lifelong love of science, art, and storytelling. After earning my degree, I spent time traveling, creating, and exploring new ways to connect with people through visual and functional design.

From painting and sewing to illustrating and now designing digital experiences, creativity has always been central to who I am. I combine my scientific mindset with artistic expression to craft user-centered, educational, and impactful designs that are both engaging and accessible.

I’m passionate about creating intuitive solutions that improve people’s lives—especially in healthcare and education. Thank you for stopping by! I’m excited to connect and collaborate on meaningful projects that make a difference.
